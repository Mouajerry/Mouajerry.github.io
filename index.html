About Me


My name is Jerry Moua. Jerry Moua is a Team Lead/Supervisor at Carquest/Advance auto parts in Shawnee, Kansas. He has been in his position for one year but has been with the company for eleven years. When he isn't at work he spends his time with his daughter, family, and friends. Some of his hobbies include playing on the guitar, reading, networking, happy hours, and bing watching shows on Netflix or Hulu. He is currently studing Coding and Web design at Kansas University.
